How does Tsunami send my products to potential customers ?

Once you have deployed your Tsunami platform, you can add your contacts online and group them according to their preferences. The platform also lets you publish your offers and products online: Photo, description, cost. Tsunami walks through your contacts and uses their preferences to choose and send them products they may like. They receive your offer by Mail (and SMS). The SMS comes with the name of your company as sender. The mail is well designed, with your logo in the header, it is Mobile phone optimized, it contains photos of your products and a button to click and go buy online directly. This process is done automatically and repeatedly, as soon as you start publishing products on your platform.
